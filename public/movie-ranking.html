TOP 5 MOVIES TO WATCH!!!

Dont Miss out!!

1. F1

A high-octane drama starring Brad Pitt as Sonny Hayes, a retired F1 driver lured back to race in a struggling team’s final bid for glory. Combining authentic on-track action with a mentorship story, it delivers adrenaline and heart, though some might find the formula familiar to classic sports redemptions.

2. Su From So (Kannada, 2025)

This Kannada-language comedy blends supernatural whimsy and small-town charm, following Ashoka, whose crush accidentally triggers rumors of ghostly possession. It's earned acclaim for its relatable humor, vibrant characters, and sharp commentary on community life, though its pacing occasionally meanders

3. Sinners

A suspenseful drama about twin brothers trying to leave their dark pasts behind, only to confront new, more sinister forces in their hometown. Its blend of family tension and thriller elements makes it a standout, though the heavy tone may not suit every mood.

4. Avatar: Fire and Ash

James Cameron returns to Pandora with this visually spectacular sequel, as Jake and Neytiri’s family faces new dangers and the fierce Ash People tribe. Its epic scope and advanced special effects are impressive, though the runtime and emotional stakes demand audience commitment.

5. Superman

James Gunn reinvigorates the superhero icon, exploring Clark Kent’s balancing act between his alien heritage and human values in a more cynical era. This reboot offers a contemporary lens on heroism—with sharp humor and nostalgia—but changing the classic formula may divide longtime fans.
